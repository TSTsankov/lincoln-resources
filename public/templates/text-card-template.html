What is disclosure?

Disclosure is making known a disability to the university. It involves discussing difficulties you may encounter, any reasonable adjustments needed for attainment and individual examination arrangements.

How do you disclose?

You can disclose at any time during the recruitment process, such as on your application or UCAS form or when you are on your course.

You can also disclose a disability through contacting the Disabilities Team using Connect2 or emailing: disabilityadvisor.ss@coventry.ac.uk
